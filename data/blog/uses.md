A running list of the hardware and software I use.
Inspired by the `/uses` pages on [uses.tech](https://uses.tech/), this is my own version.
I will keep updating it as things change.

## Editor & Terminal

- **Editor:** VS Code, mostly stock settings for now.
  I'll come back and fill in theme/extensions once I settle on a setup I like.
- **Terminal:** Windows Terminal.
- **OS:** Windows 11.

## Hardware & Desk Setup

- **Keyboard:** [NuPHY Air60 V2](https://nuphy.com/products/air60-v2), a 60% wireless mechanical keyboard.
  Running some custom layouts on it, more detail on that coming later.
- **Mouse:** Logitech MX Master 2S.
- **Monitors:** Dual monitor setup, stacked vertically (one on top of the other) instead of side by side.
  I find looking up and down easier than side to side.
- **Desk:** Standing/height-adjustable desk.
  I keep a walking pad under it to get steps in during the day.
- **Homelab:** An old laptop repurposed as a home server, running Plex and Docker, with a 4TB NAS attached for file storage.

## Apps & Software

- **PowerToys:** Microsoft's Windows utility suite, for window management and other quality-of-life tweaks.
- **Ditto:** Clipboard manager, useful for storing and reusing snippets.
- **Redgate SQL tools:** For working with SQL Server/SSMS, snippets and productivity features.

## Other Gear

- **Headphones:** Bose QuietComfort, going on 6 years strong.
- **Phone:** iPhone 13 mini, just retired an iPhone SE to get here.

## While Coding

Coffee of choice: coffee, with cream and sugar.
Not fancy, just how I like it.
